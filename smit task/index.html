About Us

NextGen Circuits is the premier hub for students focused on the future of technology. We specialize in hands-on projects involving **coding, electronics, and hardware design**. Our mission is to cultivate young leaders who can not only write code but also build and innovate physical systems. We believe in practical experience over theory, preparing our members for the demands of the modern tech industry.
